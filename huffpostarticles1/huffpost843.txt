Hopefully they learned their lesson.

On “Jimmy Kimmel Live” Wednesday, folks on the street got a chance to apologize for the dumb stuff they did to their teachers.

The stunt was to celebrate Teacher Appreciation Week and it proved that educators sure do take a lot of crap.

The former student who stole his teacher’s checkbook and the guy who placed his teacher’s car on the roof still need to see the principal ― right this minute.
